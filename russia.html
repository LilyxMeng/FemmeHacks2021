Russia

Russia a transcontinetail country located in eatren Europe and Northern Asia. The largest city and Russia’s capital is Moscow. It is the largest country in the world. Russia is home to 146,238,185 people.

Food

Russian’s eat a lot of cabbage, beets, potatoes, tomatoes, bread, berries, mushrooms, fish, and meats. They also have their own pancake called Blini which is like a crepe.

Music

Russian folk music is primarily sung because in 1648, Tsar Alexis I of Russia banned instruments in the Russian Orthodox Church stating they were from the devil. But in the 19th century Vasily Andreyev started playing the Balalaika in performances small and large.

Religion

The main religion in Russia is the Russian Orthodox Christian religion. They have many different Cathedrals belonging to this religion which in themselves are wonderful works of art. The onion dome architecture of the cathedrals serve as a distinct cultural icon.
